How We Handle Disputes

At Abuja Urban Light Properties Limited, we understand that disputes can sometimes arise between sellers, landlords, and clients, and we are committed to helping our clients resolve these disputes in a fair and amicable manner. We have a well-established dispute resolution process that is designed to ensure that all parties involved are treated with respect and that the best possible outcome is achieved. Our dispute resolution process begins with a consultation with all parties involved, where we listen to each party's concerns and try to understand the root cause of the dispute. We then work to identify the key issues that need to be resolved and propose solutions that are mutually acceptable to all parties involved. If a resolution cannot be reached through consultation, we may recommend mediation or arbitration. We have a team of experienced mediators and arbitrators who can assist in resolving disputes through alternative dispute resolution (ADR) mechanisms. Mediation and arbitration are confidential, cost-effective, and less time-consuming than traditional litigation, and we have a high success rate in resolving disputes through these methods. In cases where litigation is necessary, we work with a team of experienced lawyers to ensure that our clients' interests are represented in court. We also maintain close communication with our clients throughout the legal process and provide regular updates on the progress of the case. At Abuja Urban Light Properties Limited, we prioritize transparency, fairness, and professionalism in all our dealings, and we are committed to finding solutions that meet the needs of all parties involved. Our ultimate goal is to ensure that our clients are satisfied with the outcome of any dispute and that their relationship with us is not adversely affected by the dispute.
